About the offer

Saving time and making your flying experience seamless is our priority. With our Fast Forward service, you can enjoy priority check-in and Anytime boarding.

How much does it cost?

A nominal fee of INR 600 per person per sector for domestic travel and INR 800 per person per sector for international travel is applicable if you wish to choose this Service.

* Pre-book Fast Forward service and get up to 20% off on above rates.

  8. The FFWD service is not available for connected segments, which originate from a location where IndiGo does not offer FFWD services.
  9. FFWD is non-cancellable, non-transferable and non-refundable (i.e., only FFWD services once purchased cannot be cancelled, refunded or transferable), except in case of cancellation of the flight booking by a Customer/passenger, in such cases, the charges for FFWD will be refunded.
