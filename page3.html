This discounted fare is exclusively for our doctors and nurses, who continue to battle the pandemic from the frontline. Please refer to the below mentioned terms and conditions for detailed information.

  1. Eligibility:

    Doctors and nurses (“Tough Cookies”) booking IndiGo flights through IndiGo’s website www.goindigo.in or mobile website shall be eligible for up to twenty five (25%) discount on certain IndiGo flights (“Offer”).

  2. Identification:

    • A valid Medical Council of India (MCI) or State Medical Council registration number for doctors has to be submitted at the time of booking and the original/self-attested copy of the registration certificate with a stamp of the relevant hospital or medical facility that the doctor is currently associated with along with a photo identification card, is mandatorily required to be shown by a passenger at the time of check-in

In case of a ‘no show’ or cancellation of the booking by the relevant doctor or nurse, other passengers on the same PNR will be denied boarding and may only be allowed to travel with a fresh ticket (i.e. without discount) to be purchased at the relevant airport of departure
