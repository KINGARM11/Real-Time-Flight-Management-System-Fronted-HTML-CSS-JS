About the offer

All age groups on one booking!

In our constant efforts to deliver a hassle-free experience, we are happy to announce that you can now book senior citizens along with other passengers on a single PNR. Not only this, you can also select double, triple seats for the eligible passengers from the search widget itself. After all, a family that travels together, books together as well.

Senior citizens above the age of 60 will get 6% off on the base fare.
